'I want to stay here and I will' - Fermin Lopez sends strong message on Barcelona future

He's not going anywhere

by Gill Clark

Aug 10, 2025 at 10:35 PM GMT+1

content_copy
  • comments
  • Comments

Fermin Lopez insisted he has no intention of going anywhere after starring for Barcelona in Sunday's 5-0 win over Como in the Gamper Trophy.

Advertisement

There have been whispers throughout the summer of interest in Fermin and he showed once again why clubs are interested with a brilliant display.

Lopez then spoke about his future after the match and was adamant he will stay at the club.

"It was a great match, and it helps us get into the rythym. Very happy with the work, we must continue like this."

"I want to stay here, and I will. Playing here is my dream. The new signings have been very good and will help us a lot this season."

"Marc is the first captain and we respect him a lot."

Lopez is really pushing for a starting spot at Barcelona right now and will be hoping to be in the XI when Hansi Flick's side kick off their La Liga campaign next weekend against Mallorca.
